## HOW TO ADD A NEW CLIENT ENTRY

- Disqualified: [N]
- Approved for Outreach: [N]

---

## STATUS DEFINITIONS

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Pending | On approved list, outreach not yet started |
| Outreach Sent | First message sent (awaiting response) |
| Follow-Up 1 | Day 4 follow-up sent |
| Follow-Up 2 | Day 10 final attempt sent |
| Replied | Lead responded — active conversation |
| Booked | Appointment booked |
| Showed | Appointment confirmed shown → trigger Skill 04 |
| No-Showed | Appointment missed |
| Not Interested | Lead declined — do not contact |
| DNC | Do Not Contact — remove permanently |
| Archived | No response after full sequence — cold archive |
