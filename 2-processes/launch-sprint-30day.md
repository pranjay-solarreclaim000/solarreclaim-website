# 🚀 30-Day Launch Sprint: SolarReclaim Operational Engine

## 📌 Overview
This tactical process defines the 30-day sprint to transition from research and system design to active lead reactivation and revenue generation for US Solar Installers.

**Objective:** Execute a rapid-fire lead reactivation cycle using NEPQ scripts to generate "Shown" appointments and initial billing.

## 🔄 Sustainment: Transition to Steady-State
Once the 30-day sprint concludes, the process transitions from "Sprint" to "Operational Rhythm":

1. **Weekly Strategic Review:** Every Monday, review the previous week's conversion rates (Contact $\rightarrow$ Appt $\rightarrow$ Shown $\rightarrow$ Closed).
2. **Continuous Scrubbing:** Implement a rolling lead ingestion process where new dead lists are scrubbed every 14 days.
3. **Script Evolution:** Update NEPQ scripts based on common objections encountered during the sprint.
4. **Billing Cycle:** Shift from "Sprint" invoicing to a bi-weekly or monthly billing cycle based on "Shown" appt logs.
5. **Partner Expansion:** Use the Week 4 Performance Report as a case study to onboard new partners.
