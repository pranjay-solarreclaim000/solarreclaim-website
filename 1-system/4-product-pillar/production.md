# Production Module: The Lead Factory

## 1. The Mandate
The Production module owns the "manufacturing" process of high-intent appointments. It is responsible for the mechanical execution of lead processing, guiding leads through an emotional discovery process using the NEPQ (Neuro-Emotional Persuasion Questioning) framework to move a raw lead through the pipeline to a committed state.
- **Owns:** Lead scrubbing, sequence deployment, qualifying conversations, appointment setting.
- **Forbidden:** Designing the scripts (R&D mandate) or managing the final lead handoff to the client (Fulfillment mandate).

## 2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Throughput Volume:** Number of leads processed per day/week.
- **Qualification Rate:** % of scrubbed leads that meet the "Qualified" criteria.
- **Sequence Efficiency:** Time from lead entry to appointment set.

## 3. The Resource Map
- **Tools:** Lead Scrubbing Software, Sequencing Tools (SMS/Email/VM), CRM, Qualification Checklists.
- **Roles:** Lead Processors, SDRs (Sales Development Representatives).

## 4. The Interface
- **Inputs:** 
    - Raw Lead Databases: Provided by the Customer Pillar.
    - Validated Scripts: Provided by the R&D module.
- **Outputs:** 
    - Committed Appointments: Leads who have emotionally committed to the value of the appointment and are ready for fulfillment.
    - Performance Data: Raw conversion metrics sent back to R&D for optimization.
