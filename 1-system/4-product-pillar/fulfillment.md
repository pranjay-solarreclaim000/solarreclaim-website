# Fulfillment Module: The Gold Standard Delivery

## 1. The Mandate
The Fulfillment module owns the delivery of the product (the appointment) to the partner. Its primary responsibility is ensuring the "Gold Standard" of lead quality and a seamless transition from SolarReclaim to the Solar Installer.
- **Owns:** Calendar synchronization, Lead Brief generation, Warm transfers, Partner handoff confirmation.
- **Forbidden:** Qualifying the lead (Production mandate) or tweaking the scripts (R&D mandate).

## 2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Show Rate:** % of delivered appointments that actually attend.
- **Handoff Accuracy:** % of lead briefs that contain all required data points without errors.
- **Partner Satisfaction Score:** Qualitative feedback from installers on lead readiness.

## 3. The Resource Map
- **Tools:** Calendar Sync Tools (Calendly/TidyCal), Lead Brief Templates, Communication Channels (Slack/WhatsApp).
- **Roles:** Fulfillment Manager, Account Manager.

## 4. The Interface
- **Inputs:** 
    - Qualified Appointments: Received from the Production module.
    - Partner Calendar Access: Provided by the Customer Pillar.
- **Outputs:** 
    - "Gold Standard" Lead Briefs: Detailed dossiers delivered to the installer.
    - Synced Appointments: Confirmed entries on the partner's calendar.
