# Marketing Module: Partner Acquisition

## 1. The Mandate
**Primary Responsibility:** To attract and qualify high-fit Solar Installers (B2B) and convert them into sales-qualified prospects.
- **Ownership:** B2B outbound strategy, "Revenue Recovery" positioning, lead generation for the Sales module.
- **Forbidden:** Closing contracts, negotiating commissions, or making definitive ROI guarantees without Sales/CEO approval.

## 2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Qualified Partner Leads (QPLs):** Number of solar installers meeting ICP (8-20 installs/mo, 4.2+ rating) who agree to a discovery call.
- **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA):** Marketing spend/effort per signed partner.
- **Positioning Resonance:** Conversion rate from "Revenue Recovery" outreach to booked calls.

## 3. The Resource Map
- **Tools:** LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Apollo.io, CRM (Lead Tracking), Email Automation.
- **Roles:** B2B Growth Marketer, Outbound SDR.
- **Assets:** Revenue Recovery Case Studies, ICP Filter List (TX & FL).

## 4. The Interface
- **Inputs:** 
    - CEO Strategy (Target markets, pricing targets).
    - Business Pillar (Legal compliance guidelines for outbound).
- **Outputs:** 
    - **Qualified Prospect:** A lead that fits the ICP and is primed for the "Zero Risk" pitch.
    - **Handoff Document:** Lead context (company size, current rating, known pain points).
