# After-Sales Module: Partner Retention

## 1. The Mandate
**Primary Responsibility:** To maximize the lifetime value of partners by ensuring consistent ROI and operational satisfaction.
- **Ownership:** ROI reporting, partner health monitoring, and Quarterly Strategic Reviews (QSRs).
- **Forbidden:** Promising new product features or changing pricing structures without CEO/Product approval.

## 2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Churn Rate:** Percentage of partners leaving the program per quarter.
- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR):** Growth in revenue from existing partners via expanded lead lists.
- **Partner Satisfaction Score (NPS):** Periodic feedback on the quality of appointments provided.

## 3. The Resource Map
- **Tools:** ROI Dashboards, Communication Channel (Slack/WhatsApp), Reporting Tools.
- **Roles:** Account Manager / Partner Success Manager.
- **Assets:** Quarterly Review Template, ROI Report Format, Partner Feedback Survey.

## 4. The Interface
- **Inputs:** 
    - **Signed Partner:** From Sales Module.
    - **Performance Data:** From Product Pillar (Appointment numbers, close rates).
- **Outputs:** 
    - **Retention Signal:** Confirmation of partner satisfaction (or red flags for CEO).
    - **Expansion Opportunity:** New dead lead lists or referred partners.
