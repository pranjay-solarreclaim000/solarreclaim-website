# Sales Module: Partner Closing

## 1. The Mandate
**Primary Responsibility:** To convert qualified marketing prospects into signed, committed partners using the "Zero Risk/Found Money" framework.
- **Ownership:** The sales pitch, contract negotiation, closing, and initial onboarding setup.
- **Forbidden:** Changing the core performance-based pricing model without CEO approval.

## 2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Close Rate:** Percentage of Qualified Partner Leads (QPLs) that sign the partnership agreement.
- **Time to Close:** Average days from first discovery call to signed contract.
- **Average Deal Value:** Projected revenue based on the partner's dead lead database size.

## 3. The Resource Map
- **Tools:** Zoom/Google Meet, Digital Signature Tool (e.g., DocuSign/Pandadoc), CRM.
- **Roles:** Account Executive / Closer.
- **Assets:** "Zero Risk" Pitch Deck, Standard Partnership Agreement, Objection Handling Script.

## 4. The Interface
- **Inputs:** 
    - **Qualified Prospect:** From Marketing Module (with handoff document).
    - **Legal Templates:** From Business Pillar (Legal module).
- **Outputs:** 
    - **Signed Partner:** A legally bound partner ready for lead reactivation.
    - **Partner Profile:** Verified database size, agreed commission terms, and onboarding checklist.
