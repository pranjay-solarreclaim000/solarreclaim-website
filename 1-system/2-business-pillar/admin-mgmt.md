# Admin & Management Module

## 1. The Mandate
**Responsibility:** The Admin/Mgmt module owns "Organizational Order." It is responsible for the maintenance of company records, the synthesis of data into actionable dashboards, and the systemic reporting of business health across all pillars.
**Forbidden:** It is strictly forbidden from making strategic pivots or changing the business model; its role is to report on the *current* state of the model.

## 2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Reporting Latency:** Time elapsed between data generation and dashboard availability.
- **Data Accuracy Rate:** Percentage of reports that match verified source-of-truth data.
- **Record Compliance:** Percentage of company records (contracts, licenses, etc.) that are up-to-date.

## 3. The Resource Map
- **Tools:** Business Intelligence (BI) tools, Dashboarding software (e.g., Looker/Tableau), Document Management System (DMS).
- **Human Roles:** Administrator, Data Analyst.

## 4. The Interface
- **Inputs:** 
    - All Pillars: Raw KPI data and activity logs.
    - Finance: Revenue and expense data.
- **Outputs:** 
    - CEO: High-level strategic dashboards and quarterly health reports.
    - All Pillars: Performance feedback loops based on reported metrics.
