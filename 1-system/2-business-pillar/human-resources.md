# Human Resources Module

## 1. The Mandate
The Human Resources module is responsible for the identification, acquisition, and integration of high-performance talent. It owns the definition of ideal candidate profiles, the recruitment pipeline, and the onboarding experience.
**Strictly Forbidden:** Making final hiring decisions without CEO approval; communicating offer letters to candidates without founder review.

## 2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Time to Hire:** Average days from job posting to signed offer.
- **Onboarding Velocity:** Time from Day 1 to first "Qualified Appointment" generated (for SDRs).
- **Retention Rate:** Percentage of new hires remaining active after 90 days.

## 3. The Resource Map
- **Roles:** 
    - Recruiter (Contract/Part-time)
    - Onboarding Specialist
- **Tools:**
    - Applicant Tracking System (ATS)
    - LinkedIn Recruiter
    - Onboarding Checklist (Internal)

## 4. The Interface
- **Inputs:**
    - Budget for payroll/commissions (from Finance)
    - Headcount requirements and growth targets (from CEO)
- **Outputs:**
    - Fully onboarded, trained SDRs and Closers (to Product/Customer pillars)
    - Signed employment agreements (to Legal/Finance)
