# Intelligence Module: Market Edge & Competitive Strategy

## 1. The Mandate
The Intelligence Module is responsible for maintaining SolarReclaim's competitive advantage by monitoring market trends, tracking competitor movements, and establishing feedback loops that inform product and sales strategy.
- **Strictly Forbidden:** Relying on anecdotal evidence for strategic pivots; making market assumptions without verified data.

## 2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Intel Freshness:** Monthly updates to the Competitor Matrix.
- **Feedback Loop Velocity:** Time from "Market Signal" (e.g., new competitor offer) to "Product Adjustment" (e.g., updated script).
- **Regulatory Alertness:** 0 missed regulatory changes affecting lead reactivation in TX & FL.

## 3. The Resource Map
- **Tools:**
    - Competitor Tracking Spreadsheet / Database.
    - Regulatory Monitoring Alerts (Legal/Gov feeds).
    - Customer/Partner Feedback Logs.
- **Roles:** Market Analyst / Strategist.

## 4. The Interface
- **Inputs:** 
    - Real-world feedback from the After-Sales Module (Customer Pillar).
    - Regulatory constraints from the Legal Module.
    - Lead conversion data from the Product Pillar.
- **Outputs:** 
    - Strategic pivots for the Product Pillar (R&D) to optimize scripts and offers.
    - Competitive intelligence reports for the Sales Pillar to refine the "Zero Risk" pitch.
