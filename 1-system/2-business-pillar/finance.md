# Finance Module

## The Mandate
Capture revenue, manage the $75 show fee, and track 3-5% closed deal commissions. Ensure the "Lead-to-Cash" chain is airtight.

**Strictly Forbidden:**
- Initiating lead production for any partner without a verified billing account and payment method on file.
- Releasing commission payouts without a verified "Closed-Won" confirmation from the partner.

## KPIs
- **Collection Rate:** % of "Shown" appointments successfully invoiced and collected.
- **DSO (Days Sales Outstanding):** Average time from appointment "Show" to cash in bank.
- **Commission Accuracy:** % of commission payouts matched exactly to verified closed deals.

## The Resource Map
- **Tools:** 
    - Invoicing/Accounting Software (e.g., QuickBooks, Xero).
    - Payment Gateway (e.g., Stripe) for automated show-fee collection.
    - Commission Tracking Spreadsheet/Dashboard.
- **Roles:** 
    - Bookkeeper (Fractional) for monthly reconciliation.
    - Finance Lead (Internal) for payment auditing.

## The Interface
**Linear Waterfall Position:** Second Stage (Legal $\rightarrow$ Finance $\rightarrow$ Ops).

- **Inputs:** 
    - Signed Partner Agreement (from Legal).
    - "Shown" Appointment Logs (from Product/Fulfillment).
    - "Closed-Won" Deal Notifications (from Partner).
- **Outputs:** 
    - Active Billing Account (Trigger for Operations to start lead flow).
    - Monthly Revenue Reports.
    - Commission Payouts.

## The Linear Waterfall: Legal $\rightarrow$ Finance
Finance will NOT initiate the setup of a billing account, request payment methods, or issue invoices until Legal has provided a **Signed Partner Agreement**. This ensures no revenue is chased without a binding contract in place.
