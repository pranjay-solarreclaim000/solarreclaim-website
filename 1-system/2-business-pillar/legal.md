# Legal Module

## The Mandate
Shield the company from liability, manage Partner Agreements, and ensure strict compliance with TCPA (Telephone Consumer Protection Act) and GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation).

**Strictly Forbidden:**
- Sending any outbound communication without a verified, signed Partner Agreement.
- Approving leads that do not meet the documented compliance scrubbing standards.

## KPIs
- **Contract Completion Rate:** % of onboarded partners with a fully executed legal agreement.
- **Compliance Audit Pass Rate:** % of lead batches passing the internal TCPA/GDPR scrubbing check.
- **Liability Exposure:** Number of open disputes or legal challenges.

## The Resource Map
- **Tools:** 
    - Digital Signature Platform (e.g., PandaDoc, DocuSign) for contract execution.
    - Compliance Scrubbing Software for lead validation.
    - Legal Document Repository (Vault).
- **Roles:** 
    - Legal Counsel (External/Fractional) for contract review.
    - Compliance Officer (Internal) for auditing lead sources.

## The Interface
**Linear Waterfall Position:** First Stage (Legal $\rightarrow$ Finance $\rightarrow$ Ops).

- **Inputs:** 
    - Partner Company Details (from Sales).
    - Lead Source Documentation (from Product).
- **Outputs:** 
    - Signed Partner Agreement (Trigger for Finance to set up billing).
    - Compliance Certification for lead batches.

## Dispute Resolution: Evidence-Based Logic
To maintain a "No-Argument" relationship with partners, all disputes regarding appointment quality or lead validity are handled via Evidence-Based Resolution:
1. **The Evidence Requirement:** No dispute is entertained without a timestamped recording or CRM log of the call/interaction.
2. **The Validation Gate:**
    - **Valid Dispute:** Recording proves the lead was not "shown" or failed the "Qualified" criteria defined in the Agreement.
    - **Invalid Dispute:** Lack of recording or evidence that the appointment was missed due to partner failure.
3. **The Resolution:** 
    - If Valid: Credit is issued to the partner or fee is waived for that specific lead.
    - If Invalid: The $75 show fee remains due.
