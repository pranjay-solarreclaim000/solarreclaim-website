# Operations Module

## 1. The Mandate
**Responsibility:** The Operations module owns the "Efficiency Engine" of SolarReclaim. It is responsible for the seamless orchestration of resources, the optimization of internal workflows, and the elimination of friction in the lead-to-cash chain.
**Forbidden:** It is strictly forbidden from altering financial pricing or legal contracts without explicit approval from the Finance and Legal modules.

## 2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Workflow Cycle Time:** Average time from lead ingestion to appointment fulfillment.
- **Resource Utilization Rate:** Percentage of available capacity (human and technical) being effectively utilized.
- **Process Friction Score:** Number of manual interventions required per 100 leads.

## 3. The Resource Map
- **Tools:** Project management software (e.g., Linear/Trello), Workflow automation (e.g., Make.com/Zapier).
- **Human Roles:** Operations Manager, Workflow Architect.

## 4. The Interface
- **Inputs:** 
    - Legal/Finance: Budget constraints and compliance requirements.
    - Product: Volume forecasts and production capacity.
- **Outputs:** 
    - Product: Optimized delivery schedules and resource allocation.
    - Admin/Mgmt: Operational efficiency reports and bottleneck analysis.
